What is a Vultrix?

Alongside some rules and conventions of the species

“A vultrix is a closed species I made in my first year of college. I wanted to share this creation with all of you guys and make a family of these wonderful creatures." - Ribbonfemale

Information

A baby vultrix is called a ‘kib’.
A teenage vultrix is called a ‘vult’.
The plural form of the word ‘vultrix’ is simply that - they are a lot like sheep, or fish.
Vultrix live in packs, with a leader called an alpha. There are four alphas; Flint, Equinox, Fluxx, and Helios. A vultrix has a set of powers based on its markings and colours. They are rather large creatures who live in another dimension, and serve as guardians to their ‘bonded’ soul.
Since vultrix are a closed species, they can only be purchased from the creator, ribbonfemale/Ash/Ashley. You can buy a base from Ribbon/Ash/Ashley, and download it off furaffinity after payment, or buy a vultrix custom for $25 - $35 depending on complexity.

Main Rules

The vultrix is a closed species. This means that you cannot design or create your own vultrix - you must always purchase one from the creator, Ribbonfemale. Vultrix adopts cannot be made by anyone other than Ribbonfemale.

Trading Rules


Use of the vultrix bases is only for personal reasons - you cannot make vultrix with the intent to trade or sell. You can only trade a base made vultrix to another user that also owns the base, and not for money. Custom vultrix can only be made on the vultrix base for another user that also owns the base. To clarify, if you want to trade or make customs for a user, both parties must own the base you’re using, and you cannot take custom commissions on the base.

Habitat

A place well renowned for being the habitat of the vultrix species is known as the Vultrix Homeworld, a dimension that can only be accessed through a Vultrix’s medium. More on mediums below. Consider the Vultrix Homeworld to be a giant planet, much like a ‘Large Biome’ world in Minecraft, or Pandora from the Avatar movie.
A vultrix can live anywhere, but are mostly found in places based on their base fur colour.
White: snowy biomes, cold climates, or mountains ranges.
Black: underground in dens, like foxes, or they only come out at night.

Markings and Appearance

Vultrix are essentially giant fluffy monsters with big ears. They can also have horns, wings, fins, extra tails, and more.
Anything you can think of, chances are there will be a vultrix with it! Many vultrix have different markings based on their powers and origin story. It is believed that markings, and horns, are developed soon after the birth of a kib.

Powers

A Vultrix’s powers are originally based on their markings and colours. Many Vultrix have the same or very similar powers, stemming from their paws and their mouths.
Pink: A Pink vultrix can act like Cupid, make people fall in love, and have magical powers.
Red/Orange: Red or orange vultrix can spit lava or breathe fire. Some have wings and can live near or in volcanoes, with an immunity to heat.
Yellow: a yellow vultrix can electrocute or spark others, and can have lighting and energy powers.
Green: Commonly a poisonous or nature vultrix, green vultrix can spit venom, or grow plants, and have control over plants.
Blue: a blue vultrix commonly lives and breathes underwater, or can breathe ice or snow.
Purple: These vultrix are psychic, can read minds, have teleportation powers, or are ghosts.
Black: A black vultrix or one with black markings are usually a demonic, or a bad spirit - they can curse others, along with other powers.
White: Pure and innocent, these white vultrix are usually angels, sometimes with feathered wings. They can cure the sick or bring back the dead, including plants.
Note that there are many hybrids of Vultrix powers and markings, due to breeding. These are common powers, based on common Vultrix colours. There are many more exotic powers than just these!

Anatomy

Vultrix anatomy is just like a true mammalian creature. They were originally based on a hybrid of a cat, red panda, and a fennec fox. Vultrix have skeletons, organs, and genitalia, just like humans. They breed and give birth to their young, and feed them milk and prey from the moment they’re born. A vultrix may have horns, wings, extra tails… anything! Helios, for example, has much stronger, reinforced bones to protect against his intense banshee scream. Some vultrix may have conductor-like bones to cope with electricity powers, or much stronger paws to cope with longer claws.

Personality

A Vultrix’s personality depends on their powers and markings. In general, they behave like foxes mixed with cats. They pounce, nuzzle, growl, roar, and play with each other. They are usually very gentle and sweet, but when they get hungry they can rip their enemies to shreds!

Bonded Rules 

Vultrix are first and foremost a guardian race. They are the protectors of souls, any other creature referred to as their Bonded. A vultrix may be bonded with an anthro, a human, or another vultrix. They are allowed to be mated with their bonded, although this doesn’t always happen due to any species difference.

What’s the deal with the Necklace? 

The necklace is a common ‘medium’ for a vultrix to use to be connected to their bonded. Many mediums aren’t actually necklaces - there are options for rings, gems embedded in the vultrix’s bones, earrings, or charms. A bonded human/creature usually has a matching medium. The medium is also a use of transportation between the Vultrix Homeworld and other dimensions. If a vultrix lost their medium, they will not be able to return to the Homeworld - they’d have to get another medium from a deceased vultrix or find a natural one.

Prohibited things and Restrictions

The Vultrix species is much like any other - we have many rules and restrictions.
The main rule is based on the two alpha brothers, Flint and Equinox, and the alphas Fluxx and Helios. These names are off limits to any other vultrix, as well as their most recognisable marking, the rings on their shoulders. A beta vultrix may have one ring, but this is to be obtained by permission from an alpha.
No Vultrix may have the power to drain life from others, or have instakill strength.
Beta Vultrix also have rules. There are many restrictions for betas, and vultrix of a lower class.
No other vultrix/beta may have the power to drain colours from others and use them as attacks.
Vultrix are not allowed to be overpowered, unless they are of Alpha status.

Class System and Hierarchy

Vultrix live in tight-knit packs with an alpha and maybe one or two betas. An alpha has extraordinary power, and it is very difficult to take down. A beta is stronger than the average vultrix, and even then it takes up to four or five betas working together to take down an alpha.
As Vultrix live together in packs, a family of vultrix with one individual power may be powered up. For example, a family of vultrix that live in a volcano may have much stronger fire powers than others who live alone. This is another reason to which many vultrix choose to live alone, or are cast out - they are much too powerful.
